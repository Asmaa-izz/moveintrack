JMC / real-time tracking services

Journey Management Center: roles and responsibilities

Our JMC monitors journeys in real time and ensures compliance with safe driving standards. Using live feeds from the vehicle monitoring system together with phone contact with drivers, we verify that safe driving practices are being followed.

Aligned with objectives including ensuring driving policies are implemented (for example IOGP-style frameworks) and continuous improvement in driving behavior.

Operational programs

Examples of how the control center adapts to seasons, risk geography, and en-route events—drawn from live journey management practice.

Seasonal journey planning

During winter or storms we reinforce driver awareness with notifications: check brakes after standing water, reduce speed, and increase following distance.

Ramadan and high-risk areas

Targeted driving plans for exceptional periods, plus additional approvals and assessments where routes carry higher driving or security risk.

En-route intervention

Critical alerts such as harsh braking, overspeeding, panic alarms, no-go zones, and accidents flow to the center where operators intervene; major events trigger structured emergency response and post-incident analysis.
